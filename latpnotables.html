This is a preliminary list of notable people of Latino/a descent who are lesbian, gay, bisexual, gay or lesbian-like, friends of the family (heterosexuals who have made contributions to gay or lesbian causes or worked with gay/lesbian themes), and those on whom more research is needed. Non-Latino/as, who have produced work on Latino/a issues, are identified with an * asterisk next to their names. NOTE:This list is not intended to "out" anyone, but is meant to honor all who have worked to promote the visibility and well-being of lesbian/gay Latinos/as. Inclusion on this list does not necessarily identify a person as gay or lesbian.

Two biases apparent in this compilation are that Latinos/as from the United States in general and California in particular are strongly represented, while those living in Latin America have been slighted except insofar as they are mentioned in sources available in the U.S. Further, the political difficulties and dangers of coming out in many Latin American societies force many to remain closeted despite the important contributions they have made.
