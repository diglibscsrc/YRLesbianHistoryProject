LIVING WITH PRIDE: RUTH ELLIS @ 100.

Produced and Directed by Yvonne Welbon

Information about ordering the film can be found at: www.sistersinthelife.com Link

Yvonne can be reached at: ywelbon@nwu.edu

Living With Pride is a one hour documentary about the life and times of Ruth C. Ellis. In addition to exploring her rich past, her life today as an advocate for senior and lesbian rights is an important focus of the video. Living With Pride traces Ruth's efforts to bring technological and social change into both her senior and lesbian communities and offers a rare opportunity to experience a century-long history of African American lesbians through the life lived by one inspiring woman.
