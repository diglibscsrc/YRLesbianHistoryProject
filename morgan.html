Lesbian Photographers List

The following is a list of lesbians who were listed in the LESBIAN PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTORY,
edited by Morgan Gwenwald, NYC: self-published, 1982.
The book included 71 photographers, plus 2 lists: one of 48 lesbian periodicals and one of 9 feminist periodicals.

I am working on a lesbian photographers project, I am not in touch with most of these women, and do not know their work. Do you?
Please contact Tee Corinne: tcorinne@magick.net
