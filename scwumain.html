* WHO WE ARE
SCWU was founded in 1976 in Los Angeles. We are lesbians from all walks of life who seek in SCWU a common denominator in educational functions. We are lesbians who want a safe place to share our strengths, concerns, cultural diversity, and resources with each other, and who seek in SCWU support for our lifestyles. Our membership is composed of women who value and contribute to the community. Our aim is to eradicate negative stereotypical images of lesbians by example and through positive educational programs about our concerns, issues and lives. Our mission is to empower lesbians to achieve their potential.
