LESBIAN LEGACY COLLECTION - SUBJECT FILES

This list is made available for your convenience while it is being corrected. It contains typos, etc.

1. NOT ALL people listed here were lesbian. We collect materials on ALL women, because the history of lesbian contributions is complex and intertwined with many aspects of history.
2. Due to the shortage of funding, staff and the large number of requests by people who want to know what we have about them individually, we can no longer answer those types of questions. If you live in or are visiting the Los Angeles area, please make an appointment to visit us.
3. We will answer research inquiries that are based on more than "what do you have about me in your files." We hope you will be patient with this.
4. We do offer formal research services and this work is charged on a sliding scale. e-mail: retter@skat.usc.edu
