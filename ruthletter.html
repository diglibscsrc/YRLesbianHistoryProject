LETTER TO FRIENDS OF RUTH/RUTH'S SCHEDULE OF APPEARANCES

August 20, 1999

Dear friends of Ruth Ellis,

Thank you for sending email to Ruth. Currently she does not have her own computer, so we print out her emails and she reads them the old fashioned way.

She has received hundreds in the last few months, and feels pretty overwhelmed by all the attention she is getting. But she reads every one, and enjoys telling people that she gets email.

For those of you who have been forwarding fun things to her, we will need to ask you to remove her from your "bulk mail" list. It is a problem to get copies of mass mailings that are not to her individually. Her eyes are not good, and we don't want to strain them with extra reading. We appreciate your attention to taking care of this.

Of course she is not able to respond to all this mail she gets, but she does want to thank people for thinking of her. She's been very busy this year!

To order her film, call 1-800-343-5540 or send $30 + $5 s/h to Our Film Works, PO Box 267848, Chicago, IL 60626, or go to www.sistersinthelife.com

You can mail cards or donations for Ruth's "Travel" Fund to Ruth Ellis, PO Box 76, Okemos, MI 48805-0076. (Ruth lives on the minimal SS income, and is very appreciative of any and all donations. Please make checks payable to Ruth Ellis.)

Ruth thanks you again for your messages.

Sincerely, Sarah Uhle for Ruth (A friend, and her "secretary")

RUTH WILL BE AT THE FOLLOWING EVENTS:

Washington, D.C. for the Black Gay Pride March there Muncie, IN for the National Women's Music Festival where she did a workshop and answered questions for over an hour; San Francisco, CA where her film won the audience award for Best Documentary and she spoke to over 40,000 Lesbians at the Dyke March.

Detroit, MI - July, 1999 - she had a number of birthday parties and special events in Detroit

Hart, MI - August 12-15 she was camping out at the Michigan Women's Music Festival in Hart, Michigan. Hundreds of women got to see her film and thousands got to see her dancing on the night stage.

Chicago, IL - August 28 & 29th she'll be at the Chicago premier of her film

Springfield, IL - Sept. 19th the local Springfield, IL lesbian group will be hosting an event to celebrate her birth there over 100 years ago. She'll attend a reception and film showing co-sponsored by the local lesbian group and the historical society.

Toronto, Ontario, Canada - She will not be able to attend the Toronto Film Festival, but Yvonne and the film will be there for a great crossover opportunity!

New York, New York - December 10th a planned trip for Ruth and Yvonne including a film premier and other events

February, 2000 - Atlanta, GA a planned trip for Ruth and Yvonne including a film premier and other events

June, 2000 - Providence, RI Ruth hopes to attend the annual Golden Threads conference.
