LESBIAN PHOTOGRAPHERS EXHIBIT


"Lesbian ConneXion/s."

This is an international traveling lesbian photo exhibition with the theme: "Lesbian Lives, Lifestyles and Visibility." A second theme is "Once the World Will Know Who We Are." More than 60 photographers from 14 countries are taking part. The exhibition started last year in Amsterdam during the Gay Games. This year in March, the exhibition will be held in Gent, Belgium. We are also making a catalogue from the exhibition. As part of the exhibition, we have organized a large program with workshops, lectures, performances...
