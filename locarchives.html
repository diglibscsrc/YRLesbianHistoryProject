* Lesbiana Latina Archives
Yolanda Leyva (UA/History) is establishing the first Lesbiana Latina Archives. She is interested in collecting any types of materials relating to Latina Lesbians. The archives is also associated with the ongoing oral history project undertaken by Leyva in relation to her own work on the Tucson lesbian community. If you have any materials, please contact Yolanda Leyva, yleyva@mail.utep.edu or at 915-747-7067.
