still here Committee Quotes

"Sisterhood has aways welcomed me to read from my latest writing project, and has opened its doors to my students, my colleagues, and writers in the anthologies I've edited. I know whenever I read there that the audience will be receptive and informed; they come because they care about women's words and ideas. Sisterhood has supported me since the beginning."--Terry Wolverton

The power of Sisterhood Bookstore is really about the fusion of culture and activism."--Terry Wolverton (introducing Maxine Waters as winner of the first annual Sisterhood Bookstore Community Hero Award)

"Sisterhood Bookstore has been an ongoing resource for my creativity, and personal and professional growth, and has never failed to offer support to me, as a feminist and psychotherapist, in the ongoing struggle against sexism and patriarchy."--Linda Levinson

"I wish the whole world operated like Sisterhood--with good feminist politics; small friendly atmosphere; dedication to women and women's 'community'; and the opportunity to partake of the creativity of thousands of women, through their stories and ideas."--Irene Wolt

"It's been a great and dynamic pleasure to work with this committee. I fervently support independent bookstores. The day we do not have independent bookstores is the day that censorship is the norm in society. It is crtitical that we continue to fight for our independents, the bookstores, writers and artists."--Lisa Hartouni
