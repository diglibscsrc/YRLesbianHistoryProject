Regular Series Events:

Women's Book discussion group continues in its fourth year on the third Thursday of the month at 6:30pm. All women are welcome to join us for interesting reading, discussion, and a chance to make new friends. Please phone 310-477-7300 to find out the next book selection.

Lesbian book discussion group meets on the fourth Tuesday of the month at 6:30pm. All women are welcome. Join with other women who have a passion for reading thought-provoking and stimulating books by and about lesbians. Discussions are open and informal and provide an opportunity to meet women who share similar interests. Phone 310-477-7300 for the next book to be discussed.

Chess club for women and girls led by chess master Ivona Jezierska meets the last Sunday of the month at 2:00pm. Join us for chess instruction, chess talk and chess playing. Women and girls at any level from absolute beginner and up are welcome. Chess has too long been dominated by men, but the times are changing. Be part of the intriguing, challenging world of chess. Please RSVP at 310-477-7300.
