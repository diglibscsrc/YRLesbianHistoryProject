HOW TO ORDER BOOKS

We are proud of our selection of books, music and videos by and about women in the areas of literature, history, psychology, feminist theory, gender, lesbian, bi and gay studies, health, cooking, children, art and more. If you are looking for a book in our specialty, we probably have it. If not, we can get it as quickly as any other online bookstore. We can mail-order it to you via USPS, global priority or UPS. Please telephone us at 1-800-747-0220 or 310-477-7300. You can send us a check or give us your Visa, Mastercard or Discovery card number. We are open seven days a week from 10:00am to 8:00pm Los Angeles time.
